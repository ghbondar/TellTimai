The Story of Ancient Timai, by Isabel Zermani

Published by Nahdet Misr, The Excavation at Tell Timai by the University of Hawaii, The National Geographic Society, The Lounsbery Foundation, and Evelyn Leslie-Silverstein publication

Current Research in Egyptology XV Proceedings

Supervisors Dr. Sean Winter, Colleen Westmor, and Courtney Bobik wrote an article about their findings from the 2011-2013 Seasons titled, “Food Consumption During the First Century BCE at Thmuis.” Courtney Bobik represented the group and presented the article at the Current Research in Egyptology (CRE) XV conference at the University College of London and King’s College London in April 9-12, 2014. The proceedings for the conference were edited by Dr. Massimiliano S. Pinarello, Justin Yoo, Dr. Jason Lundock, and Dr. Carl Walsh and were published by Oxbow Press.
